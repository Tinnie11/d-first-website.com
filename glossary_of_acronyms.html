Glossary of Acronyms

  • BSN: Bachelor of Science in Nursing

    The BSN is an undergraduate degree program that prepares students for a career in nursing. It provides comprehensive training in nursing theory, clinical skills, and patient care.

  • NCLEX: National Council Licensure Examination

    The NCLEX is a standardized exam that nurses must pass in order to obtain licensure to practice nursing in the United States and Canada.

  • RN: Registered Nurse

    Registered Nurses (RNs) are healthcare professionals who have completed a nursing program and obtained licensure to practice nursing. They provide and coordinate patient care, educate patients and the public about various health conditions, and provide advice and emotional support to patients and their families.
