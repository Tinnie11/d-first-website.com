What drew you to the field of nursing?

Initially, my journey into nursing wasn't a deliberate choice, but rather a familial suggestion during a time of uncertainty. However, as I reflect on my path, I've come to appreciate the unexpected trajectory that led me here. While nursing wasn't my initial passion, my time as a student nurse has gradually ignited a sense of purpose within me. Despite the challenges, I've discovered a genuine interest and commitment to becoming a globally competent nurse. Though the journey has been unconventional, I've found a newfound sense of fulfillment and purpose in the field of nursing.

How did Aklan State University become your choice for education?

Aklan State University wasn't initially on my radar, but fate had other plans. Through a rigorous admissions process, I found myself humbled and grateful for the opportunity to join ASU's esteemed BSN program. While the university may not have initially overwhelmed me, it has since become a nurturing environment where I've found my academic footing. Despite its quirks, ASU has provided me with a supportive community and a rigorous curriculum that has exceeded my expectations. I am grateful for the quality education and sense of belonging that ASU has provided me on my nursing journey.

Could you talk about the experiences that shaped who you are today?

As I enter my second year of pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Nursing, I reflect on the transformative experiences that have shaped my journey thus far. From navigating the transition to college life to overcoming academic challenges, each obstacle has contributed to my personal and professional growth. Despite moments of doubt and uncertainty, I've embraced the lessons learned from clinical instructors and peers, understanding that constructive feedback is instrumental in my development. Through resilience and self-reflection, I've embraced the journey of molding myself into the nurse I aspire to become.

How did you cope with academic stress during your studies?

Coping with academic stress during my studies has been a journey of trial and error. I've found solace in establishing a balanced routine that prioritizes self-care, including regular exercise, adequate sleep, and mindfulness practices. Additionally, seeking support from peers and faculty members has been invaluable in navigating challenging coursework. Embracing healthy coping mechanisms and maintaining perspective on the bigger picture has helped me manage academic stress effectively.

Any practical tips for someone in the midst of a nursing degree?

For those amidst a nursing degree, I encourage you to prioritize self-care and establish a support system to navigate the challenges ahead. Embrace opportunities for hands-on learning and seek guidance from experienced professionals. Remember to celebrate your accomplishments, no matter how small, and maintain a growth mindset as you progress through your journey. Most importantly, never lose sight of the profound impact you will have on the lives of others as a compassionate and skilled nurse.
